## 🎯 Website Summary
The website of the Faculty of Informatics at the University of Debrecen serves as the primary online presence for the faculty. It provides information for prospective students, current students, faculty members, and the general public. The site details educational programs, research activities, faculty profiles, news, events, and administrative information. The Faculty of Informatics aims to provide quality education, competitive knowledge, and international career opportunities in the field of informatics.

## 📄 Content Overview
The website's content is organized into several key sections:

*   **Homepage:** Features a slideshow with important announcements and links, recent news, and quick links to major sections like Prospective Students, Education, Digital University, and Beyond Education.
*   **Prospective Students:** Provides information on scholarships, benefits, rankings, and an overview of the faculties and educational programs offered.
*   **Education:** Details the various faculties and institutions within the university, as well as the different degree programs offered in English (Foundation, Undergraduate, Graduate, PhD/Doctoral).
*   **Digital University:** Highlights digital resources and platforms available to students, including the UD Studyversity App, Neptun, E-Map, and E-Learning.
*   **Beyond Education:** Showcases other aspects of the university, such as the Clinical Centre and Institutes for Agricultural Research and Educational Farm.
*   **Media:** Offers access to videos and photo albums related to the faculty and university.
*   **Contact:** Provides contact information for the Faculty of Informatics, including phone, email, address, and links to department and general phonebooks.
*   **Events:** Displays a calendar of upcoming events.
*   **News:** Features recent announcements and articles relevant to the faculty and university.
*   **Formal:** Includes links to useful resources, document libraries, and information on data protection and privacy policies.

Content types include text, images, links to external and internal resources, and a calendar. The site also provides information on tuition fees for various programs.
